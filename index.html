THE COLLECTOR OF "CRYSTALS"!!!

INSTRUCTIONS:

You will be given a random number (See: "No. to Match") to begin the game.

You see those four pictures of "Crystals" (You see what I did there?) below that random number? You're supposed to click on a picture - each click will add a specific amount of points (ranging between 1 and 12) to your total score.

The object of the game is to match your total score to said random number. You lose the game if your total score goes ABOVE the random number. So click carefully.

Oh, and here's the kicker -- you won't know the amount of points each "Crystal" is worth until you click on it. So, again -- click carefully.

And, one more thing: the values of each "Crystal" resets after each new game.

Let the games begin.
